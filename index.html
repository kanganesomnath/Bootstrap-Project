Our Work

Education

The Satyshiv Foundation (TSF) works in education by providing free learning opportunities to underprivileged children. It helps improve literacy through books, digital resources, and guidance. The NGO also supports school enrollment, reduces dropout rates, and encourages equal education for all children, especially in rural and economically weaker communities.

Healthcare

The Satyshiv Foundation (TSF) contributes to healthcare by organizing medical camps, spreading awareness about hygiene, and providing basic health services. It helps poor people access medicines and treatment. The NGO also focuses on preventive care, nutrition, and educating communities about maintaining a healthy lifestyle for better living conditions.

Youth Development

The Satyshiv Foundation (TSF) promotes youth development by offering skill training, career guidance, and personality development programs. It encourages young people to become confident, responsible, and self-reliant. The NGO also supports employment opportunities and motivates youth to actively participate in social and community development activities.

Women Empowerment

The Satyshiv Foundation (TSF) works for women empowerment by providing skill-based training, legal awareness, and health education. It helps women become financially independent and confident. The NGO supports women in starting small businesses and promotes equal rights, safety, and participation of women in society.

Agriculture

The Satyshiv Foundation supports farmers through sustainable agriculture practices, training programs, and resource access, helping improve crop yields, promote eco-friendly methods, and ensure long-term livelihoods for rural communities.

Environment

The Satyshiv Foundation works to protect the environment through tree plantation drives, waste management initiatives, and awareness programs, promoting sustainability, reducing pollution, and encouraging communities to adopt eco-friendly practices.
